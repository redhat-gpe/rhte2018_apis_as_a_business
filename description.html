APIs As A Business

This multi-product lab has you build an insurance quote platform using Red Hat's Agile Integration suite of products. The insurance quote platform exposes REST APIs that facilitate trade between your customer and its partners.

The lab is deployed on Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform and includes the following components:
  • Red Hat Fuse Online
  • Red Hat 3scale API Management
  • Red Hat Single Sign-On
  • Red Hat Decision Management
  • Apicurio
  • Microcks
